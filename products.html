Meat Department

Dale's takes pride in quality sourcing, product variety, old world butcher's handwork and outstanding value pricing. Most conventional supermarkets cut corners in one of these areas, but not Dale's. We run our Meat Department the same way they used to in the old days - because we know how good it can be when things are done right. After all, we shop here, too! Custom cuts available upon request.

Meat Selection

  • Beef: We start with USDA Choice Beef. Our skilled hometown butcher "reads the grain" and then prepare cuts to exacting specifications, delivering great quality at terrific values.

  • Pork: For a healthy alternative to red meat, try Dale's All-Natural Pork. We take the same pride and craftsmanship in procuring and preparing pork as we do with our Choice Beef.

  • Poultry: We always sell top quality (Grade A) all-natural poultry raised cage-free in the United States, and our hand selection and packaging system ensures that you get the best of the best... and the freshest, too!

Gourmet Deli and Prepared Foods

Every day is a new day! At Dale's we're all about making your life simple. Our ourmet deli offers hot and cold prepared entrees and sides for lunch using our own recipes. We carry premium brand cold cuts, cheeses and house made salads along with a varity of specialty items.Our food is prepared with the finest ingredients from within our store, so you get the freshest-tasting food.

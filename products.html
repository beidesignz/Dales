Our Products

We are committed to high-quality food products, with an emphasis on fresh perishables, premium meats, convenient and delicious prepared foods with an uncompromising commitment to customer service.

Meat Department - "Hometown" Butcher is on site

Dale's takes pride in quality sourcing, product variety, old world butcher's handwork and outstanding value pricing. Most conventional supermarkets cut corners in one of these areas, but not Dale's. We run our Meat Department the same way they use to in the old days in our new state of the art facility - because we know how good it can be when things are done right. After all, we shop here, too! Custom cuts available upon request.

Meat Selection

  • Beef: We start with USDA Choice Beef. Our skilled hometown butcher "reads the grain" and then prepare cuts to exacting specifications, delivering great quality at terrific values.

  • Pork: For a healthy alternative to red meat, try Dale's pork. We take the same pride and craftsmanship in procuring and preparing pork as we do with our Choice Beef.

  • Poultry: We always sell fresh Grade A chicken delivered and packaged daily ensuring that you get the best of the best!

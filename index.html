Welcome to Dale's Market

Our stores, located in Branchville, Blairstown, and Andover have been family owned and operated since 1971. Our focus is food: fresh produce, in-house butcher-cut meats, and daily prepared meals. We offer convenience, quality and excellent customer service.

New Garden Center!

Our newest location in Andover is now open. We have loaded our greenhouses with beautiful plants and flowers. Annuals, perennials, herbs, vegetables and more. We have partnered with some incredible local growers. We remain committed to offering the highest caliber of plants and flowers for all of your gardening needs.
